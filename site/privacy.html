Privacy Policy

Tire Rotation Tracker is an offline-first app. All entries you create are stored locally on your device in the app's Application Support directory. We do not collect, transmit, or sell any personal data. There are no analytics, no third-party trackers, and no network requests made by this app other than Apple's own StoreKit calls needed to process an optional Pro purchase.

If you delete the app, your locally stored data is deleted with it.
